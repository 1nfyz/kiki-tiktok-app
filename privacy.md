# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 31, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how **ArtistBot** ("we") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our desktop application.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect minimal data necessary for the functionality of the bot:
- **Authentication Data:** TikTok Access Tokens and Refresh Tokens (stored locally on your device/server).
- **Log Data:** Technical logs generated during the operation of the bot for debugging purposes.

**We do NOT:**
- Collect your passwords.
- Store your personal videos on our servers (they are transferred directly from your device to TikTok).
- Share your data with third parties.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To authenticate your TikTok account via the TikTok Login Kit.
- To upload content to your profile via the Content Posting API.
- To improve the stability of the application.

## 4. Data Storage
All sensitive data (tokens) is stored locally in your environment (e.g., `.env` file or local database). You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time.

## 5. Third-Party Services
Our application uses the TikTok API services. By using our app, you acknowledge that your data is processed by TikTok in accordance with [TikTok's Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/us/privacy-policy/en).

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
